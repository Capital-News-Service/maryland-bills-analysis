Around 1/3 of the bills introduced this session were passed

BY JAKE GLUCK | CAPITAL NEWS SERVICE
A total of 2497 pieces of legislation were introduced in the 2019 Maryland General Assembly. 1124, or 45 percent, made it to a final floor vote in at least one of the chambers. Of these, 866, or 77 percent, were passed into law. In total, 35 percent of the bills introduced this session became law. This data includes joint resolutions.

Source: LegiScan and a Capital News Service analysis
